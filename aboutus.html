Who We Are

We are a non governmental community-driven initiative dedicated to empowering girls through education, mentorship,health education and advocacy. Our mission is to break barriers and create opportunities for the girl child.We work to improve their quality of life through inclusive health education,scholarships, care and support to the vulnerable communities across Kenya.

OUR MISSION

To empower girl children through access to education, healthcare, mentorship, and life-changing opportunities, enabling them to reach their full potential.

OUR VISION

To create a society where no girl is limited by her circumstances and every girl has equal opportunity to succeed.
